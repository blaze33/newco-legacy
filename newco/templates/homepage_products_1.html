Documentation for the "template trial tool"



  • What the "template trial tool" does :
    • You can create templates that the team will be able to view, without touching the visible part for visitors (here you can make trials on the "default" branch)
       
    • Team can view and access the templates from the "nav bar"


       
  • What you have to do to create trial templates :
    • Create a template in the same directory as the original: "original_template_<Template_Name>.html"

       

    • If "template trial tool" not implemented in your view:

      • Views.py:

        • Add:
          `from utils.views.trial_template import TrialTemplateMixin`

        • Add the class `TrialTemplateMixin` to your view's arguments
